To deliver a concise, interactive executive summary dashboard that tracks profit trends, sales distribution across different market segments, and geographical performance, enabling rapid identification of key revenue drivers and areas of concern.

### 1. Profit Trend Analysis

* **Profit by Month and Year (Line Chart):** This chart tracks the sum of profit over time, covering the period from September 2013 to November 2014.
    * **Key Insight:** Highlights significant profit fluctuations. Peaks are visible around **September 2013** ($1.6M) and **November 2014** ($2.0M), indicating strong seasonal or event-driven performance. Troughs typically hover around $0.5M to $0.6M.
* **Filters:** Time-based filters for **Year** (2013, 2014) and **Month** allow for detailed investigation into specific periods.

### 2. Geographic Profit Distribution

* **Total Profit by Country (Map Visualization):** This map displays the total profit generated across different countries and regions, represented by the size of the bubble.
    * **Key Insight:** Major profit contributors are located in **North America** and **Europe**. The specific countries (e.g., United States, various European nations) are not labeled but are clearly localized on the map.

### 3. Sales by Product and Segment

* **Total Sales by Product and Segment (Stacked Column Chart):** This chart compares the total sales generated by five different products across five distinct market segments.
    * **Products Analyzed:** Paseo, VTT, Velo, Amarilla, and Carretera.
    * **Market Segments:** CHANNEL PARTNERS, ENTERPRISE, GOVERNMENT, MIDMARKET, and SMALL BUSINESS.
    * **Key Segment Insight:** The **ENTERPRISE** segment (dark blue) is the dominant sales driver for most products, particularly **Paseo** ($14.9M) and **VTT** ($9.3M).
    * **Product Performance:** **Paseo** generates the highest overall sales ($14.9M, largely from Enterprise), while **Velo** and **Amarilla** also show strong performance.
